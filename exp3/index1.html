About Us

The sole purpose of this web application is tracking the Expiry of the particular batch so as to reduce the wastage and carry out timely transport.

Organic foods are grown so that we get the required nutrients in their natural forms. So it is very much necessary to look after their storage process more carefully.

Our web application, ensures that the organic products do not last or remain past their manufacturing date.
And the farmers, sales office personnels , have the details of Every batch of products in one click. i.e easy to access.

Our web application is a contribution in this market, which might be turn out a product very helpful for people who sell organic products as well as for the ones who buy.
